We are runway models

Who we are

A modern agency based in the heart of Dublin City. We represent Actors for film, television and stage; and Models for editorial, catalogue and show, both in Ireland and internationally. We provide casting solutions and cast management for advertising, commercial and digital, including Child Models and Family Casting.

Become a model

If you are 5’8″ or above (Women) / 6' or above (Men) then please send us one headshot, one profile, one smiling, and one full length photo with your contact details, age, height, bust, waist and hip measurements to info@morgantheagency.com We do not require professional photos, camera phone digitals are fine. Please NO FILTERS, NO TAN, NO MAKEUP, NO SELFIES. Natural photos where we can see your skin and hair clearly. We do our best to reply to all submissions but due to the volume we receive it is not always feasible.

Our story

Morgan The Agency is the longest established International Model Agency in Ireland.Morgan started life as Nan Morgan Models in 1973, the brainchild of industry guru Nan Morgan. In the early 80's Nan recruited her daughter Rebecca, taking her away from the advertising industry. Over the years, in its quest for perfection, the agency expanded from Nan Morgan to Morgan Brand and finally to Morgan The Agency, increasing our portfolio of models and business substantially. Morgan The Agency went from strength to strength in all areas, including the International arena where we forged strong relationships with agencies across the globe becoming the first port of call for clients coming to Ireland to work and source new talent. We now have a wide representation of talent dotted around the globe.

Services

Morgan The Agency provide a complete service supplying Models, Photographers, Fashion Stylists, Make-up & Hair Artists, Actors, Commercial talent, Influencers and Personalities, to a broad and ever expanding list of clients. Spotting, encouraging and developing talent is at the core of our agencies ethos, the mainstay of our success.
