Become a model

If you are 5’8″ or above (Women) / 6' or above (Men) then please send us one headshot, one profile, one smiling, and one full length photo with your contact details, age, height, bust, waist and hip measurements to info@morgantheagency.com We do not require professional photos, camera phone digitals are fine. Please NO FILTERS, NO TAN, NO MAKEUP, NO SELFIES. Natural photos where we can see your skin and hair clearly. We do our best to reply to all submissions but due to the volume we receive it is not always feasible.
